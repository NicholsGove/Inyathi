Our Story

Building Healthier Communities Through Reliable Supply

Inyathi-Mz was established with a clear purpose: to bridge the gap in pharmaceutical and medical supply across Mozambique. We recognized that healthcare facilities were struggling with inconsistent supply chains, substandard products, and unreliable distributors.

Today, we are proud to serve hospitals, clinics, pharmacies, NGOs, and government health institutions across the country. Our team of experienced professionals brings deep knowledge of the pharmaceutical industry and a genuine commitment to improving healthcare access.

We partner exclusively with certified manufacturers and authorized distributors to ensure every product we supply meets the highest quality standards and complies with Mozambique's healthcare regulations.

Quality Assurance

Our Commitment to Quality

At Inyathi-Mz, quality is not just a standard — it is our foundation. We partner only with certified manufacturers and authorized distributors who meet our strict quality criteria.

Our supply chain is structured to maintain product integrity at every stage, from procurement to delivery, including proper storage conditions and temperature-controlled logistics.

  • Products sourced from WHO-certified manufacturers
  • Full cold chain management for temperature-sensitive products
  • Regular quality audits and compliance checks
  • Compliance with Mozambique's MISAU regulations
  • International quality certifications maintained
