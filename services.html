What We Offer

Comprehensive Supply Chain Solutions

Pharmaceutical Distribution

We provide reliable, nationwide distribution of prescription medicines, OTC drugs, vaccines, antibiotics, and specialty pharmaceuticals. Our distribution network covers all major provinces in Mozambique.

  • Prescription & OTC medicines
  • Vaccines and biologics
  • Chronic disease medications
  • Pain management drugs

Bulk Supply & Procurement

We offer cost-effective bulk purchasing solutions for large healthcare institutions, NGOs, and government programs. Our procurement team negotiates the best prices from certified suppliers.

  • Volume-based pricing
  • Government tender support
  • NGO program supply
  • Flexible payment terms

Cold Chain Logistics

Our cold chain infrastructure ensures temperature-sensitive products maintain their integrity from storage to delivery. We use validated cold storage facilities and refrigerated transport.

  • 2°C–8°C refrigerated storage
  • Validated cold transport
  • Temperature monitoring
  • Cold chain documentation

Medical Equipment Supply

We supply a wide range of medical equipment, diagnostic tools, surgical instruments, and hospital consumables from certified manufacturers to equip healthcare facilities.

  • Diagnostic equipment
  • Surgical instruments
  • Hospital consumables
  • Laboratory supplies

Regulatory Compliance Support

We ensure all products comply with Mozambique's MISAU regulations and international standards. Our compliance team handles all necessary documentation and certifications.

  • MISAU regulatory compliance
  • Import documentation
  • Product certification
  • Quality audit support

Dedicated Customer Support

Our experienced team provides responsive, personalized support for all your supply needs. From order placement to after-sales service, we are always available to assist.
